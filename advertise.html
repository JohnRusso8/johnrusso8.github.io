How it Works

Promote your bar, restaurant, or venue directly to nearby users on Barchive. Ads start at $50 and scale based on two factors you choose:

  • Radius: how close someone needs to be to your location to see the ad
  • Duration: how long the ad runs

At the end of your campaign, you’ll receive a report showing how many people clicked your ad. When someone taps it, they’re taken straight to your place page in the app—where they can view posts, ratings, and more.
