Barchive Bar Giveaway FAQ

Barchive giveaways are designed to bring extra energy into your bar, engage customers, and help more people discover your venue through the Barchive Social app.

What is a Barchive giveaway?

A Barchive giveaway is an in-person event where we bring a prize wheel, promote your bar, and give customers a fun reason to interact with your venue and the Barchive app.

How much does this cost the bar?

Nothing. Barchive covers the cost of the giveaway items and handles the setup.

What does the giveaway entail?

Barchive brings in a prize wheel and runs a giveaway inside the bar. Customers can enter for a chance to win a larger prize and spin the wheel for smaller prizes, creating an easy and fun way to engage guests during the event.

What does the bar need to provide?

Usually very little. We just need approval to run the giveaway, a good spot to set up the wheel, and any guidance from your team on where we should be located during the event.

Do we need to staff the giveaway?

No. Barchive handles the giveaway setup and runs the wheel. Your staff can continue focusing on normal service.

What is the goal of the giveaway?

The goal is to bring extra energy into the bar, get customers engaging with the venue, and help promote the bar through Barchive.

When do giveaways usually happen?

We typically prefer weekend nights because that works best for our team and usually has the most customer traffic, but we can work around what makes the most sense for the bar.

How long does it run?

Most giveaways run for a few hours, depending on the event, crowd, and what works best for the venue.

What kind of prizes are included?

Barchive can cover giveaway items such as gift cards, drinks, merch, or other small prizes depending on the event setup. If the bar wants to include its own merch or specials, those can be added too.

How does this help the bar?

It gives customers another reason to engage, creates content and buzz around the venue, and helps get more people discovering the bar on Barchive.

Do customers need the Barchive app to enter?

The giveaway is designed to help customers discover and use Barchive while engaging with your bar. We can explain the app to guests during the event and help them get started.

Interested in hosting a giveaway?

Reach out to Barchive and we’ll help coordinate a setup that works for your bar, your crowd, and your schedule.
