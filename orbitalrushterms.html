Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy describes how Orbital Rush: Space Run ("the Game," "we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and shares information when you play our mobile game on iOS and Android.

By downloading, installing, or using the Game, you agree to this Privacy Policy.

1. Information We Collect

Information You Provide

  • Username: When you create an account or set a display name for leaderboards, we store the username you choose.

Information Collected Automatically

  • Anonymous User ID: An anonymous identifier is generated by Firebase Authentication so we can save your game progress, high scores, and unlocked content across sessions.
  • Game Data: Your high scores, total coins earned, equipped skin, upgrades purchased, missions completed, and other in-game progress.
  • Device Information: Device model, operating system version, language, country, and unique device identifiers (such as Advertising ID / IDFA).
  • Usage Data: Crash reports, app session length, and gameplay events for analytics purposes.
  • Advertising Identifiers: On iOS, we may request permission to use the IDFA (Identifier for Advertisers). On Android, we use the Advertising ID. These help our ad partners deliver more relevant ads.

Information We Do NOT Collect

  • We do not collect your real name, email address, phone number, or physical address.
  • We do not collect payment information directly (handled by Apple App Store and Google Play).
  • We do not access your contacts, photos, microphone, or location.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To save and sync your game progress across devices.
  • To display your username and score on leaderboards.
  • To serve advertisements (including rewarded ads and interstitials).
  • To analyze gameplay and improve the Game.
  • To detect and prevent fraud, abuse, or technical issues.

4. Apple App Tracking Transparency (iOS)

On iOS 14.5 and later, the Game will display a prompt asking whether you allow tracking. If you choose "Ask App Not to Track," ads will still be shown but will not be personalized using the IDFA. You can change this setting anytime under Settings → Privacy & Security → Tracking.

5. Children's Privacy (COPPA)

Orbital Rush is rated for a general audience and is not directed to children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child under 13 has provided personal data, please contact us and we will delete it.

6. Your Data Rights

You have the following rights regarding your data:

  • Access: Request a copy of the data we hold about you.
  • Deletion: Request that we delete your account and game data.
  • Opt-out of Ad Personalization: Use your device's ad settings (iOS: Settings → Privacy → Tracking; Android: Settings → Google → Ads).

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at the email address listed at the bottom of this page.

7. Data Retention

We retain your anonymous user ID, username, and high score data as long as your account remains active. If you uninstall the Game without requesting deletion, your high scores may remain on the leaderboard indefinitely under your chosen username.

8. International Users

Your data may be processed in the United States or other countries where our service providers operate. By using the Game, you consent to this transfer.

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The "Effective Date" at the top of this page reflects the latest revision. Continued use of the Game after changes constitutes acceptance.
