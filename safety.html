BarFriends is committed to protecting children and preventing any form of child sexual abuse and exploitation (CSAE). We have zero tolerance for content, behavior, or users that facilitate or promote CSAE.

We prohibit any form of CSAE-related content, including images, text, or interactions.

Users found engaging in such behavior are immediately banned and reported to authorities.

Reporting & Enforcement

Users can report inappropriate content or users directly in the app.

Reports are reviewed promptly and acted upon within 24 hours.

We cooperate with law enforcement and organizations like NCMEC when necessary.
